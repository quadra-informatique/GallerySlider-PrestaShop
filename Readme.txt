READ ME

This module display a carousel in the center column on the homepage.

The images are uploaded in the module through the back office.

You have the possibility to resize the images and by the same way the size of your carousel (which depends on the size of your images).

You can also choose either to display the thumbs on the top or on the right of the big images by using the checkbox <Vertical Display>.

You must save your configuration after changes.
